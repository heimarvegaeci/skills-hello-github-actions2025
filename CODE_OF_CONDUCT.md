# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our learning community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive in discussions
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them learn
- Providing constructive feedback
- Focusing on learning and growth
- Being patient with questions and mistakes

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling, insulting, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct inappropriate for a learning environment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainers directly.
